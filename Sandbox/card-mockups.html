  • Encourages engagement through variety
  • Best for organizations with flexible involvement

  • Shows requirements upfront
  • Protects both volunteers and beneficiaries
  • Prevents wasted time for unqualified users
  • CTAs appear after qualification confirmed
  • Best for organizations with strict requirements
